Banana Bread

The things you are going to need:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup of margarine
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 3 ripe bananas mashed

Preheat the oven to 350°F, and grease a bundt pan.

In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Mix margarine into the mashed bananas.

In another mixing bowl, mix the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour. Combined.

Pour the batter into your prepared loaf pan. Bake for 45 minutes to 1 hour at 350°F, or until a tester inserted into the center comes out clean.

Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for a few minutes. Then remove the banana bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving. Slice and serve.

Pumpkin Pie

The things you are going to need:

  • 1 cup of pumpkin
  • 1 tsp of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 tsp of ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp of salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup of milk or cream

Preheat oven to 450°F.

Combine all ingredients together.

Pour into unbaked pie shell.

Bake for 15 minutes at 450°F. Reduce temperature to 350°F and bake for 40 minutes or until cooked through.

Apple Crisp

The things you are going to need:

  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup of flour
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 3/4 cup of rolled oats
  • 1/2 tsp of cinnamon
  • 5-6 large apples

Preheat oven to 375°F.

Remove skin from apples and cut in thin slices.

Add sliced apples to a square baking dish with 1 tbsp of water.

Mix dry ingredients together and spread over apples.

Bake for approximately 45 minutes or until apples are soft and top is crisp and brown.
