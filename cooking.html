Chicken Bacon Alfredo

The things you are going to need:

  • 8oz fettuccine noodles
  • 1 1/2 cups cooked chicken, diced
  • 1 tsp of minced garlic
  • 1 cup of cheese
  • 1 cup of cream
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled cooked bacon
  • Salt and pepper

Dice and cook your chicken. Add seasoning if desired.

Add uncooked pasta, chicken, garlic, cheese, cream and broth in a sauté pan.

Cover and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.

Cook for 12-15 minutes, stirring frequently, until pasta is cooked.

Add in salt and pepper to taste.

Stir in bacon and serve.

Shephard's Pie

The things you are going to need:

  • 1 to 1 1/2 pounds of hamburger
  • 1 onion chopped
  • 1 clove of garlic minced
  • 4-6 medium potatoes mashed
  • 1-2 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce
  • Kitchen Bouquet browning sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of flour mixed into a small jar of water
  • Vegetable of your choice

Preheat oven to 350°F.

Brown the ground beef, add the onion and garlic until cooked. Stir in the flour/water mixture and let thicken for a couple minutes.

Add Worcestershire sauce and some kitchen bouquet until meat is a nice brown colour.

Transfer meat into an oven ready casserole dish or glass pyrex dish.

Transfer meat into an oven ready casserole dish or glass pyrex dish.

Top with mashed potatoes and sprinkle with paprika.

Bake for 45 minutes.

Chili

The things you are going to need:

  • 2 lbs Hamburger
  • 2 cans tomato sauce (15oz cans)
  • 2 cans kidney beans with liquid
  • 2 cans pinto beans with liquid
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced green chili
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 3 medium tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 cups water

Brown beef & drain.

Combine all ingredients.

If you are using a regular pot bring to a simmer over low heat and cook approximately 2-3 hours, stirring every 15 minutes. You can also use a crockpot (my preference), 6-8 hours on the low setting.
